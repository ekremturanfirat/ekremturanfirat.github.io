Who am I?

Hi,

am Ekrem Turan Fırat. Let me briefly introduce myself. I am a first year (currently) student of Electrical and Electronics Engineering. Although I graduated from a technical high school, I also have a maker background dating back to my middle school years. Right now, I am not a maker but an engineer candidate, and I am doing jobs that I think are nice.

I don't want to explain my CV to you here. You must have seen and examined it in the introduction, but let me briefly tell you. I am studying at Cumhuriyet University. I will soon go to AGH University of Science & Technology in Poland for my education. I am also actively working as an Undergraduate Researcher at METU Power Lab.

Okay, that's enough CV :)

I am a young person who is trying to improve himself especially in power electronics and related hardware design. I opened this website to help those who want to improve themselves in this and similar fields like me (And a portfolio :))

I am not someone who hides what I know, especially in technical matters. On the contrary, I want to share it without applying any filters to help others. I know I am not an expert but as a student I have been interested in these for a very long time. I am trying to learn how to learn. I also have certain work experiences. I do not promise to give you information that will create wonders but I can help you on how to get started.

Here I want to share the information I have gained especially on PCB Design and Power Electronics with its sources. I want to touch on topics such as how to do a project, how to research, how to proceed, how to finalize. Here I will not try to share hearsay information like on some platforms. Frankly, I do not know if you will read it but I will do write when I find time.

You can always reach me about the mistakes I made or any other issue from my contact information on the homepage. I will try to get back to you within a few hours.

Best regards, see you in the next article.
Stay open source.
